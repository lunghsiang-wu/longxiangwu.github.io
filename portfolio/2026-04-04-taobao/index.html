Data cleaning

  1. Retain orders within buy_amount[1,1000].
  2. Check whether there are any abnormal values missing values and deal with them.
  3. If the attribute of auction_id in trade is not specified, we will change it to item_id by default.
  4. The properties in the data set are all numbers. You need a corresponding dictionary to know what attributes correspond to. Delete them first.
  5. Change day to date form.
# Keep the record within buy_mount[0,1000]
trade = trade[(trade.buy_mount >= 1) & (trade.buy_mount <= 1000)]
# Column rename
trade.rename({"auction_id": "item_id"}, axis=1, inplace=True)
# Take out the property for the time being and put it aside, and then analyse it later.
property = trade.property
trade.drop('property', axis=1, inplace=True)

Analysis of repurchase rate

# Product category repurchase rate
# Group according to the product category, and then index the circular category to find the repurchase rate of each category.
t = trade.groupby(by=['cat1', 'user_id']).size()
purchase_dict={}
for i in trade.cat1.unique():
    c = t.loc[i].value_counts()
    purchase_dict[i]=((c.sum()-c[:1])/c[:1]).values[0].round(4)
plt.figure(figsize=(10,6))
sns.barplot(x=list(purchase_dict.keys()),y=list(purchase_dict.values()))
plt.title("Repurchase rate of major categories",fontdict=fontdict)
plt.show()

The repurchase rate of each product category is also extremely low, not reaching 1%. Considering that most of the users’ single purchases are the same and the repurchase rate is low, it shows that users’ desire to buy back a single product is extremely low. Merchants should consider from the product perspective, such as product quality and shopping experience.

Project summary

  • May and November every year are the peak sales periods, which shows that the promotional effect of the shopping festival is obvious and should continue to be maintained;
  • The upward trend of the total number of users and total sales reflects that the influence of the store on the market is gradually expanding, and there is still a market space to be developed, but the speed is slowing down;
  • Due to the special nature of baby products, the life cycle of users is limited, so the business focus of the store should not only improve the repurchase rate, but also not ignore the acquisition of new users;
