1 Data description and import

The data set used in this case is credit card customer data. The specific data includes: the number of each customer case, the customer’s credit card limit, gender, education level, marital status, age, monthly repayment status during the data recording period (April-September 2005), billing records and payment records, in order to And the record of whether the customer breached the contract in the next month. The table information of the specific data field is recorded as follows:

Column name Meaning description Data type
ID Customer ID. int64
LIMIT_BAL The credit limit given by the bank to customers, including the personal credit limit and the customer’s family credit limit. float 64
Sex The gender of the client. Males are written as 1 and females are 2. int64
Education The customer’s education level. Graduate students and above are recorded as 1, universities are recorded as 2, high school students are recorded as 3, and others are recorded as 0. int64
MARRIAGE The client’s marital status. Unmarried is recorded as 2, married is recorded as 1, and others are recorded as 0. int64
Age The age of the customer. int64
PAY_1～PAY_6 These six variables are the monthly repayment records from April to September 2005, with values of -1 to 9, representing the cumulative number of overdue months. int64
BILL_AMT1～BILL_AMT6 These six variables are the monthly billing records from April to September 2005, that is, the monthly credit card consumption records. float 64
PAY_AMT1～PAY_AMT6 These six variables are the monthly payment records from April to September 2005, including the amount of bill repayment and the amount deposited on the credit card. float 64
default.payment.next.month On behalf of whether the customer will breach the contract next month, the breach of contract shall be recorded as 1, and if there is no breach of contract, it shall be recorded as 0. int64

6 Conclusion

The final classification effect obtained in this case is not satisfactory. The best AUC value is about 0.8, and the highest F1 value obtained from the positive sample is 0.55. For scenarios such as credit card default, which requires high recall rate of positive samples, higher recall rate and accuracy are often required. However, due to data limitations, it is difficult for this data set to achieve a good classification effect on the final results, but the processing and reference of different algorithms and indicators are worth reviewing.
